Play as Guest

The biggest barrier between a new visitor and their first Norn was the sign-in wall. You had to create an account before you could see the game at all. Starting with v0.7.0, there’s a “Play as Guest” button right on the gate screen. One click and you’re in — generating a planet, watching species populate your world, and meeting your first AI villagers.

Guest sessions are fully functional: you get the same world generation, the same creatures with real genetics and emergent behavior, the same Divine Chat. The only difference is your progress isn’t saved between sessions. A gentle nudge appears after a few minutes offering to create an account so you can keep your village.

This release also includes the first fully working production build. Previous versions had asset path issues that prevented the game from loading outside development. That’s fixed — the game now loads cleanly at its production URL with zero console errors.

Play at play.multiversestudios.xyz/mvee.
