BREACH.MMO v2.6 — 2026-06-10

Try It. Earn It. Share It.

v2.6 closes the loop between “I heard about this hacking sim” and “I'm an Analyst Pass holder who convinced three colleagues to sign up.” The Security+ curriculum path is already there. This release adds the mechanics that get people into it and back out into the world carrying proof they did.

Free-tier players now get one full Security+ mission before the paywall. Not a preview screen. Not a locked tooltip. A real SY0-701 domain mission — the same content Analyst Pass holders get. Complete it, and the game asks if you want to keep going. If you do, here’s the path.

When you finish all five SY0-701 domains, you earn a shareable badge at play.multiversestudios.xyz/breach/badge/sy0701/<username>. Paste that URL into LinkedIn, Discord, or Twitter and it unfurls with a full card — your handle, your badge, your proof. It’s not a screenshot. It’s a live page with Open Graph meta that renders everywhere link previews work.

Analyst Pass holders get a referral code. Format is BREACH-USERNAME-XXXX — deterministic, yours, visible in your dashboard. When a player you refer activates their Analyst Pass, you earn 500 XP. The referral chain is tracked. Repeat referrers will accumulate XP fast.

  • Trial mission gate — one free SY0-701 mission for every new player, no account required
  • Security+ Ready badge — shareable OG card at a permanent URL, earned by completing all 5 domains
  • Referral code system — BREACH-USERNAME-XXXX codes, 500 XP per conversion
  • Social proof — live count of players who earned Security+ Ready this month on the landing page

The game is pay-what-you-can. If you’ve been meaning to try it: play.multiversestudios.xyz/breach/.
