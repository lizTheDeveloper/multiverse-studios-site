Cultures of the Belt v0.11.0 — Physics Gets Real

May 9, 2026 Cultures of the Belt 200 commits

The Belt just got a lot more physical. v0.11.0 replaces the old spring-approximation physics with a proper impulse-based rigid body system. Cables are real Verlet integrations with tensile strength. Asteroids collide and shatter. Rubble fields require entirely different mining strategies than solid bodies. And when you finally fill a cargo hold, you can watch your hauler make the long transit to a planet and deliver it.

Highlights

  • Voxel Physics Overhaul — real impulse dynamics, Verlet cables, destructible asteroids, fragment scattering on collision. No more voxels accelerating to lightspeed.
  • Planet Delivery — dispatch resources to planets, watch transit ships cross the system map with glow rings, track deliveries in the new HUD panel.
  • Commodity Markets — prices now affect your production rates. Sparkline charts in the market panel. The economy breathes.

We also landed 20+ balance fixes (factory costs, fusion prerequisites, fuel ratios) and hardened save/load stability. The full changelog is in the repo.
