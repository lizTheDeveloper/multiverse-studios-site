March 28, 2026 CotB v0.8.1

Second Rock

The outpost system has been in Cultures of the Belt for a while. Claim Beacon tech, mass driver return trajectories, multi-base resource networks — the infrastructure was there. The problem was that most players never found it. You had to know it existed to look for it.

v0.8.1 fixes the discovery problem.

Outpost discoverability

Unlock the Claim Beacon technology and every asteroid you click now shows an Establish Outpost button — cost, projected yield, balance impact, all right there. No digging through menus. When the tech completes, a one-time HUD hint tells you what just became possible. The system map marks your outposts with diamond icons, dotted lines showing mass driver return trajectories back to base.

Your first base is where you survive. Your second rock is where you start to think about what you're actually building.

ARIA's morning reports now include per-outpost ore delivery summaries — how much came from where, what the delivery lag looks like, where your throughput is constrained. Running a multi-base operation is a different kind of game. Now you can actually see it.

Cultures of the Belt is free to play. Pay what you can if you like what you find in the rocks.
