Precursors v0.4.29: Dance, Sprites, Songs

March 25, 2026 Precursors

This patch brings group choreography to Norns. The new [dance] action lets creatures dance together, triggered by songs playing nearby. When one Norn starts dancing, others join in — visible, emergent social behavior that makes watching them feel alive.

On the admin side, two new tabs landed: Visual Appearance gives sprite coverage stats, a phenotype viewer, animation filmstrips, and hue previews for every species. Species Songs adds a full song browser with modal editing, MP3 upload, and orphan detection to keep the music catalogue clean.

We also bumped the cloud save body limit to 6MB, fixing sync failures for players with large save files.
