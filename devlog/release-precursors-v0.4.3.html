v0.4.3 — Recipe Book + Journal Fixes

2026-03-21 Precursors Patch

This patch adds the Recipe Book — a new panel that tracks every crafting recipe your creatures have discovered. When a Norn crafts something, you'll see a toast notification confirming what was made.

We also fixed the journal. It was filling up with repeated "First Breeding" entries every time the page reloaded, making it read like... well, not the kind of journal we intended. Milestone tracking now persists properly, and the label has been softened to "Bonded."

Finally, a subtle but important fix: word learning was silently broken due to a punctuation mismatch in the vocabulary tracker. Norns were speaking but never recognizing words they'd already learned. That's now fixed — vocabulary should accumulate naturally over time.

Changes

  • Recipe Book panel with discovered crafting recipes
  • Craft event toast notifications
  • Journal no longer spams breeding entries after reload
  • Word learning punctuation bug fixed
