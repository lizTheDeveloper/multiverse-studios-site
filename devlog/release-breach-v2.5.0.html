BREACH.MMO v2.5 — 2026-06-09

Security+ SY0-701 Curriculum

v2.5 ships the reason BREACH.MMO exists: a structured path from “I want to learn security” to “I am ready for the CompTIA Security+ exam.” Fifteen hand-curated missions cover all five SY0-701 exam domains — Threats & Vulnerabilities, Architecture, Implementation, Operations, and Governance — mapped directly to official CompTIA objectives 1.1 through 5.2.

The missions are not flashcard quizzes. Security Policy Challenge puts you in front of a real compliance control matrix and asks you to select the right policy under a specific standard — with a writeup that explains exactly which SY0-701 objective you just practiced. Threat Classification gives you raw threat intel and asks you to attribute the actor, identify the ATT&CK tactic, and defend your reasoning. Incident Response walks you through a PICERL-aligned breach — containment actions have consequences, and sequencing them wrong is an outcome, not an error screen.

A new SY0-701 Domain Tracker in your dashboard shows progress across all five domains. Complete every domain and you earn the Security+ Ready badge. The curriculum is gated behind the Analyst Pass — free-tier players see the landing page and the domain map, so the scope is visible before you commit.
