March 25, 2026 Precursors

This patch changes how young Norns move through the world. Before they can speak, they communicate differently — a curious head tilt when approached, a shy retreat from loud sounds, a reaching gesture toward the Hand. These feel more alive than text ever did.

Under the hood, we've tuned how creatures change across generations. Something is shifting in the population — not all at once, but in a way that rewards patience. We won't tell you what to look for. You'll know it when you see it.
