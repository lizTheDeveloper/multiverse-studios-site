Precursors v0.4.30: The Cognitive Cusp

March 25, 2026 Precursors

This patch lays the foundation for Precursors' generational evolution arc. Young Norns at low cognitive tiers no longer burn LLM tokens on gurgles and coos. Instead, pre-cognitive animation triggers drive their physical responses — a curious head tilt when approached, a shy retreat from loud sounds, a reaching gesture toward the Hand. These feel more alive than text ever did.

Under the hood, cognitiveCapacity genetics have been tuned for the three-generation arc: Generation 1 starts near the cognitive cusp and may speak their first words by elder stage. Generation 2 inherits that potential and begins speaking by adolescence. By Generation 3, Norns are reading and teaching. The evolutionary timeline now feels like discovery rather than a switch being flipped.
