BREACH.MMO v1.7.0 — 2026-06-03

Player Experience Update

v1.7.0 ships three features that make the game feel less anonymous: you now have a profile, your technique usage gets tracked, and new players get a guided path into their first match.

Player Profiles — hit the PROFILE button in the nav to see your rank, match history, win rate, and XP. Your profile is shareable at /players/<username> — send it to friends or drop it in your resume. The ATT&CK technique stats section will fill in as you run exploits and the data accumulates.

Technique Tracking — every MITRE ATT&CK technique you execute in a match now gets logged to your profile. The technique heatmap shows your attack surface over time — what you default to, what you've never touched. It's both a learning tool and a flex.

Onboarding Polish — new players now get a pre-match skill assessment that recommends an exploit path, in-match callout cards the first time you see the CU counter and terminal (dismissible), and a tutorial match with an explicit win condition before you enter real matchmaking. Veterans skip all of it.

  • Player profiles — rank, match stats, XP, technique heatmap
  • Public profile URL — shareable at /players/<username>
  • ATT&CK pipeline — per-match technique usage persisted and displayed
  • Skill assessment — recommends first exploit path at match start
  • First-match HUD cards — contextual callouts on CU, terminal, objective
  • Tutorial win condition — guided 1v1 vs bot unlocks real matchmaking
