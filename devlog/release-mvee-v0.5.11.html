v0.5.11 — Feed Yourself

v0.5.9 gave starving villagers a crude survival instinct: till the nearest grass and hope. It worked, but it was a hardcoded fallback — a reflex bolted onto the side of the behavior system rather than flowing through it naturally.

This patch replaces that workaround with a proper context-based decision in SeekFoodBehavior. When a villager can’t find food, they now evaluate their surroundings — available soil, nearby water, existing farms — and transition to farming as a deliberate choice, not a last-ditch override. The behavior tree stays clean. The villager stays fed.
