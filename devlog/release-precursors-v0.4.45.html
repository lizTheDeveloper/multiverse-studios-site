Precursors v0.4.45: Ship Repairs and Audio Fixes

March 26, 2026 Precursors

This release brings a new damage narrative system to the ship. When the vessel takes its first hit during interstellar travel, the event triggers repair songs and teaching interactions between creatures. Damage becomes a storytelling moment, not just a number going down.

We also squashed a loading bug where audio files for certain species were causing 404 errors and slowing down the loading screen. Pending audio entries are now cleanly gated, and baby sounds lazy-load instead of blocking startup.

Under the hood, the ship is becoming more aware of its passengers. We've been laying groundwork for something interesting — but you'll have to play to find out what.
