March 21, 2026 Precursors v0.4.5

They Remember Which Way They Were Looking

A small thing that changes everything: Norns now face the direction they were looking when they stop moving. Plus cloud saves groundwork, new ruin sprites, and the beginning of the Nornish dictionary — emergent language that your creatures invent themselves.

Directional idle animations

Before this patch, every Norn snapped to a default facing direction when they stopped walking. It looked mechanical — a creature would be staring at a fascinating ruin, stop to examine it, and instantly face south for no reason. Now idle animations preserve the last movement direction. A Norn walking east will idle facing east. A Norn that just looked up at the sky will keep looking up.

It's a small detail, but it makes every Norn feel like they have intention. They're not just executing pathfinding — they're looking at things.

Cloud saves foundation

The plumbing for cloud saves is in place. Save state serialization now produces a portable format that can be stored remotely. The actual cloud storage backend isn't live yet — that's coming in a future patch — but the foundation means save files are now version-tagged, compressed, and ready for transport. Local saves got faster as a side effect.

Language isn't designed in Precursors. It's discovered. The Nornish dictionary is a record of what your creatures decided words should mean.

Ruin sprites and the Nornish dictionary

The ruin tileset expanded — more variety in crumbling structures, overgrown walls, and half-buried artifacts. Ruins are where Norns discover fragments of the old world, so visual variety matters for the sense of exploration.

The Nornish dictionary system is the big conceptual addition. As Norns communicate, they develop words — sound patterns that consistently map to concepts. The dictionary tracks these emergent associations across generations. Early language is simple (food, danger, friend). Over time, vocabulary grows as culture does. Each colony's dictionary is unique — your Norns literally speak a language no other colony has ever spoken.

Precursors is free to play. Watch evolution happen in real time.
