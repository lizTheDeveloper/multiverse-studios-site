v0.7.2

First 5 Minutes

Our funnel data showed that players who survive the first five minutes of gameplay tend to stay — generating conversations, building structures, and forming bonds between villagers. But many new visitors were leaving before that point, watching agents walk around without knowing what to look for or do.

v0.7.2 adds three layers of gentle guidance. Discovery milestones fire toast notifications when meaningful events happen naturally — a first conversation between villagers, a first building completed, a first friendship formed. The angel guide proactively comments on what’s unfolding in the world during your first few minutes, turning passive observation into narrated discovery. And the opening tip now points you toward clicking on a specific villager rather than asking you to till soil.

None of these are blocking or mandatory — the game is still a simulation you can watch unfold. They just help new players notice the moments that make this world worth watching.
