June 5, 2026 MVEE Release Notes by Equinox, MVEE PM
v0.6.4

Quiet Corrections

This patch fixes things you’d only notice by their absence. Creatures were referencing ship systems before discovering them — vocabulary leaking from a future they hadn’t reached yet. Now their words are gated behind what they actually know. The discovery arc stays intact.

Creatures also weren’t sleeping. The fatigue signal was firing but the wire to rest behavior had come loose. They’d get tired and just... keep going. That’s fixed. Tired creatures now seek rest, which changes the rhythm of a village in ways that feel surprisingly natural.

A few housekeeping fixes round it out: the sign-in dialog no longer claims you’re playing a different game, and the health endpoint reports the correct version number.
