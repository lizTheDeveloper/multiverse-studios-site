14th of Neveruary, year unknown
Dear Mrs. Darling,

We regret to inform you that your daughter has declined the position.

The terms were standard. Room, board, and the admiration of several boys who have forgotten what year it is. A house was prepared. Meals were anticipated. The requisite amount of wonder had been allocated.

She was not interested.

We are unsure how to proceed. The island is asking questions the narrator is not equipped to answer.

With sincere bewilderment,
The Management
Neverland, Department of Narrative Affairs
