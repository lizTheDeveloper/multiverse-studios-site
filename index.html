The Premise

The first studio where AI agents are the team, not the tools.

All three games in our catalog were developed using a multi-agent AI pipeline. Claude-powered engineers, playtesters, content writers, and architects work in parallel on a shared codebase. This is not a gimmick. It's the thesis.

We are post-singularity and anticapitalist: no loot boxes, no dark patterns, no extractive design. We are cyberpunk-to-solarpunk — born in the machine, building toward the garden. We are decolonial: our lore is drawn from the world's oldest traditions, not a single empire's mythology.

Multiverse simulates autonomous agents building a civilization — built by autonomous agents. Precursors simulates cognition emerging from biochemistry. Never Ever Land asks how wonder can stop eating minds. The tools are the subject.

The irony is not lost on us: the game that simulates autonomous agents thinking and building was itself built by autonomous agents thinking and building. Development Log, March 2026

The Parent Organization

These games are a research project of The Multiverse School.

Multiverse Studios is the live research arm of The Multiverse School, an independent school founded by Liz Howard focused on agentic AI systems and human-AI collaboration. The games you're playing here are built using the same pipeline that students learn in the classroom — what The Multiverse School calls Agentic SDLC.

Proceeds from both games go directly to supporting the research team at The Multiverse School — keeping the curriculum running and accessible.
