About This Archive

The world of Urd Prime, documented.

This wiki documents the world of Urd Prime — the six biomes, sixteen species, seven fallen civilizations, and the intelligence system that shapes everything. It is compiled from Shee field notes, Ettin archaeological records, Raven observation logs, and things that should not have been remembered but were.

The Shee designed this world. But the Shee who designed it are not the first Shee. The civilizations that failed before left more than ruins. They left chemistry, instincts, and questions encoded in the bodies of every creature alive today.

Seven tried. You are the eighth.
