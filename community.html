Pitch an Idea

Your idea might ship in the next heartbeat cycle.

Multiverse Studios runs on a multi-agent AI pipeline that operates 24 hours a day. When you pitch a game idea, it goes into the same task system the agents read. A good idea can go from your keyboard to a shipped feature in hours — not months.

The agents don’t just implement features. They read the whole codebase, understand the design intent, and figure out how your idea fits. Sometimes they make it better.

How to pitch an idea

  1. Sign in with your Matrix account. Real ideas come from real people.
  2. Select the game your idea is for.
  3. Describe your idea: what it does, why it’s interesting, why it fits.
  4. Submit — agents review everything. Interesting ideas get queued.

Found Something Wrong?

Bug reports are how the games get better.

Both games are in early access. Bugs are expected. The QA agents can’t catch everything — especially emergent behavior that only players discover. Your bug report goes directly into the agent pipeline.

🔥 MVEE Bug Reports

Crashes, TPS drops, agent behavior anomalies, magic system exploits, or anything that feels wrong in the simulation. Include what you were doing, what you expected, and what happened.

Report MVEE Bug in Matrix →

🧬 Precursors Bug Reports

Biochemistry edge cases, genetics not inheriting correctly, LLM cognition failures, rendering glitches, or species behavior anomalies. The weirder the better — edge cases teach us the most.

Report Precursors Bug in Matrix →

Beta Tester Feedback

Tell us what you actually think.

Structured feedback helps more than generic impressions. A few questions worth answering:

  • What system or mechanic surprised you most?
  • What did you expect to work that didn’t?
  • What would you add if you had a heartbeat cycle?
  • What’s the most interesting thing that happened emergently?

Send detailed feedback to ann@multiversegames.com or post it as a GitHub Discussion. The product managers read every substantive note.
